Expected goals (xG) is the new revolutionary football metric, which allows you to evaluate team and player performance. In a low-scoring game such as football, the final match score does not provide a clear picture of performance. This is why more and more sports analytics turn to advanced models like xG, which is a statistical measure of the quality of chances created and conceded. On this site, you will find our detailed xG statistics for the top European leagues.
